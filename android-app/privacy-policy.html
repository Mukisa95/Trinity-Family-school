Privacy Policy for Trinity Online

Last updated: [Current Date]

1. Introduction

Trinity Online is a mobile application that provides access to Trinity Family School's online platform through a WebView interface. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When you use Trinity Online, you may provide information through the web application interface, including:

  • Login credentials for the school platform
  • Personal information you enter in forms
  • Communications with school staff

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Our app may automatically collect certain information:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage data and crash reports
  • Network connectivity information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide access to the school's online platform
  • Send push notifications about school updates
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Handle technical issues and provide support

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Firebase Services

Our app uses Firebase services for:

  • Push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
  • App analytics and crash reporting

Firebase's privacy policy can be found at: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 WhatsApp Integration

The app may redirect to WhatsApp for group communications. WhatsApp's privacy policy applies to any data shared through their platform.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Data encryption in transit
  • Regular security updates

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Data stored locally on your device is managed according to your device settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of push notifications
  • Uninstall the app to stop data collection

8. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for use by students and parents of Trinity Family School. We take special care to protect the privacy of children and comply with applicable laws regarding children's data protection.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or school communications.
